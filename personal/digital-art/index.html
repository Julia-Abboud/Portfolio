2020 – Present

Digital Art

A skill I started developing in 2020, I have certainly grown as an artist over the years! What started as a mild interest quickly became my favorite pastime, outlet, way to tell stories and make cool posters! I have always been a self-taught artist, studying the work of others to become introduced to new techniques but more than anything, learning from trial and error, repeated practice, and constantly pushing myself to work in different themes and styles. I have included some of my more recent pieces here and I will save the clumsy doodles that have made me better on a computer file far, far away. Digital art has improved my eye for error, encouraged me to be continuously more creative, and taught me incredible patience, focus, and diligence as one piece can take me a few days to a few weeks depending on how busy my schedule is.

One of my long-term goals is to eventually publish a graphic novel! It would be very rewarding to bring a story in my head to life. I am always open to suggestions, feedback, and conversations about digital art! Please feel free to inquire about commission work, too!

2025 – Present digital works are created using Procreate: procreate.com 2020 – 2025 pieces used the free version of Sketchbook: sketchbook.com
