Traditional Art

Digital art is my usual chosen medium, and I often import paper sketches to my computer program to make them into more refined pieces, but sometimes pencil and paper win out! There is something unique about being unable to undo your mistakes when making more traditional art. For me, this category of art includes anything physically tangible that was drawn or painted. Having only one copy makes it all the more special!
